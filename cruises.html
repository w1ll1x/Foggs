Public Cruises

We offer a variety of ticketed public cruises throughout the summer. Below is a selection of our offerings hosted on our two large-capacity vessels, the Casco Bay Cat and the UB-85. Unless otherwise noted, all cruises depart from our dock on Maine Wharf.

* All ticket sales are non-refundable unless the event is canceled, in which case all tickets will be refunded.

Featured Cruise

Portland Maine Sunset Cruise

Board the UB-85 Joseph S. Kennedy and enjoy an hour and a half cruise around the harbor throughout the summer at sunset!

Featured Cruise

Beer, Wine & Oysters Cruise

All-inclusive Beer, Wine & Oysters Cruise on Portland Harbor. Fresh local oysters on the half shell served with house mignonette and cocktail sauce. Beer and wine plus canned cocktails are included. The route passes the working waterfront, lighthouses and nearby islands, and maybe some seals!

Featured Cruise

Wednesday Night Live Music Sunset Cruise

Join us on the Casco Bay Cat every Wednesday night for live music, beautiful sunsets, and friends on this two-hour cruise. Bring your own drinks and snacks, there will be a cooler with ice provided. Live music from a local band, a restroom on board, and steady views of the working waterfront, lighthouses, and nearby islands!

Featured Cruise

Midday on the Bay

Board the Joseph S. Kennedy for a 90-minute cruise on Casco Bay, with close views of historic forts, working lighthouses and offshore islands. Optional add-ons include a Maine lobster roll with chips and fresh fruit ($40) or an arugula–goat cheese salad ($16). Local beer, wine and canned cocktails are available for purchase. For residents and visitors alike, it’s a clear, unhurried look at Portland’s shoreline!
