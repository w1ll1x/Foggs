Small Groups

Our small-group charters are designed to offer personalized experiences for groups of up to six passengers. Perfect for anniversaries, family outings, or a day of island-hopping.

$150 / hr (1-hour minimum)
After 9 PM: + $50 / hr
After midnight: + $100 / hr

Large Groups

Our two larger vessels, the Casco Bay Cat and the Joseph S. Kennedy, comfortably accommodate up to 49 passengers—ideal for weddings, reunions, and corporate events.

Under 20 guests: $300 / hr (2-hour minimum)
20 – 49 guests: $400 / hr (2-hour minimum)
After 9 PM: + $75 / hr
After midnight: + $150 / hr

Cancellations

We understand plans change. For a full refund, please provide notice at least 48 hours in advance for small-boat charters and 30 days in advance for large-boat charters. Weather-related cancellations are fully refundable or may be rescheduled at no cost.
