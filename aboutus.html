About Us

The Fogg's Water Taxi Story

Welcome to Fogg's Water Taxi and Charters, a family-owned Maine business serving the islands of Casco Bay. Our story began in 2004 when Dennis Fogg built the first Foggcraft - custom, welded aluminum alloy boats celebrated for their timeless design, smooth rides, and durability. With sons Patrick and Eben by his side, the Fogg family expanded the business, starting the water taxi in 2016.

More than just a mode of transportation, Fogg's Water Taxi and Charters offers an unparalleled experience that captures the essence of Casco Bay's beauty. Join us on one of our classic vessels for memorable journeys to the islands, where nature's wonders meet authentic Maine charm. Whether it's a public charter or a private excursion for your special moments, immerse yourself in the magic of Casco Bay with our family's cherished business.

The Foggs

Dennis Fogg

Born and raised in Maine, Dennis has spent his whole life on Casco Bay. He began boating in his youth when he and his brother lobstered out of South Freeport. After earning a degree from The Boat School in Eastport, he started Fogg's Boatworks LLC in 1984. He has grown the boat building business with his sons and eventually teamed up with them to start Fogg's Water Taxi and Charters.

Pat Fogg

Pat is a 5th generation captain. Born in Portland and raised in Maine, he spent his childhood on boats in Casco Bay. His passion for the outdoors and boats led him to earn a degree in boat building and design from The Boat School in Eastport, Maine. Today he works alongside his family designing and building aluminum alloy boats and operating the water taxi and charter company.

Elaina Fogg

Elaina left her North Carolina roots for Maine's rugged coast, trading wildlife biology for life at sea. She quickly earned her 100-ton captain's license and now captains vessels, manages operations, and helps plan charters for Fogg's Water Taxi. With skill and enthusiasm, she guides guests through Maine's scenic waters.

Eben Fogg

Eben grew up on boats in Casco Bay and graduated from the University of Southern Maine with a degree in economics and finance. His love for the outdoors and being on the water led him to join the family business, Fogg's Boatworks, and later to start Fogg's Water Taxi and Charters alongside his father and brother.

The Captains

Catherine Corey

Raised in Yarmouth, ME, Catherine grew up with Casco Bay as her playground. She earned her Captain's license to follow her passion for the water and has volunteered with the USCG Auxiliary, Sector Northern New England. Her deep connection with the bay and its island communities goes beyond taxi service.

Steve Lazaros

Steve is a USCG Master Captain and licensed Maine Guide with 40 years of boating experience. A semi-retired healthcare executive, he spends his days as a water taxi captain. He enjoys fishing and boating and lives and works on Casco Bay.

Will Jorgensen

Will grew up sailing on Casco Bay. A graduate of Portland High School, he currently attends Bowdoin College. He earned his Captain's license at 17 and is in his fourth season with Fogg's.

Paul Mentag

Originally from Michigan, Paul served five years in the Coast Guard before attending Northeastern University. After a 35-year career as a global leadership consultant, he earned his 100-ton Master's license and joined the Fogg crew in 2021. Paul loves sailing, biking, and everything water-related.

Jake Birks

Jake has lived, worked, and played on Casco Bay for 28 years. A former Coast Guard navigator, he holds a 100-ton Near Coastal Master Captain's license. He and his wife, Peggie, raised five children with a deep appreciation for the bay's beauty and power.

Chris Holt

From Brewer, ME, Chris grew up sailing the Maine coast and lakes. A Maine Maritime Academy graduate, he holds a USCG unlimited mate's license and a 100-ton Master's license. Before joining Fogg's, he served as a deck officer and navigator in the merchant marines.

Why We're Portland, Maine's Best Water Taxi

Fogg's Water Taxi stands out as the premier choice in Portland, Maine, due to our deep-rooted maritime legacy and unparalleled commitment to service. Originating from a lineage of passionate boaters, we provide a unique blend of expertise and local knowledge. Our fleet, equipped with the latest navigation tools, ensures a safe and punctual journey. Our custom boats are built specifically for guest enjoyment and feature bathrooms for your convenience. Our crew, especially seasoned sailors like Paul, brings years of experience on the water, ensuring each voyage is memorable. Whether you're planning an island adventure or a private charter, Fogg's guarantees an unmatched experience. Trust in our tradition; ride in comfort with the best.
