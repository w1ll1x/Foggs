Wednesday Night Live Music Sunset Cruise

Jun – Sep Wed Evening 🎵 Different band each week!

Join us on the Casco Bay Cat every Wednesday night for live music, beautiful sunsets, and great company on this two-hour cruise. Bring your own drinks and snacks — a cooler with ice is provided. Live music from a local band, a restroom on board, and steady views of the working waterfront, lighthouses, and nearby islands!

What to Know

  • Cruise duration: approximately 2 hours
  • Departs from Maine Wharf, 72 Commercial Street, Portland, ME
  • BYOB — bring your own drinks and snacks; cooler with ice provided
  • Live music from a local band — a different act every week
  • All ticket sales are non-refundable unless the event is canceled
