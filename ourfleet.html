Our Fleet

Our Fleet, Built at Our Boatyard

At the heart of our operations lies a legacy of craftsmanship and passion for the sea. Over four decades ago, Dennis Fogg laid the foundation for Fogg's Boatworks with a vision of building unparalleled custom boats. In 2004, this vision materialized into Foggcrafts—welded aluminum alloy boats that embody classic elegance, efficiency, and durability.

Under Dennis's guidance, sons Patrick and Eben have grown the legacy, pushing boundaries in design and innovation. Today, these boats are not only the backbone of our taxi and charter service—they also work in many roles across the bay, reflecting our commitment to quality and service. Whether you're interested in our Water Taxi, Public Charter, or Private Charter services, our custom-built boats promise an unmatched experience. For a deeper dive into our boat-building journey, visit foggsboatwork.com.
