Midday on the Bay

Board the Joseph S. Kennedy for a 90-minute cruise on Casco Bay, with close views of historic forts, working lighthouses, and offshore islands. For residents and visitors alike, it's a clear, unhurried look at Portland's beautiful shoreline.
